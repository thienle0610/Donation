ABOUT CQST

Led by four young teenagers, CQST was born in the middle of the global hunger crisis. This organization's purpose is to offer tangibles and support, especially food, to people and families with a lack of nourishments in Africa. Working with other donate organizations and companies, CQST hopes to give out the best assistance and supply for communities in need.

CURRENT CRISIS

Current situation in Africa

Currently, many of us are lucky to have a wonderful life with adequate food and drinks. However, somewhere on this planet, people are craving the necessities of life. Especially in Africa, instead of having a safe home with basic needs, 237 million sub-Saharan Africans are chronically malnourished because of economic woes, drought, and extreme weather, which is more than in any other regions ( from World Vision Organization). In the whole of Africa, 257 million people are experiencing hunger, which is 20% of the population. With the presence of COVID-19, famishment has become a global crisis. Almost half of the South African households do not have enough money to buy food due to the COVID-19 pandemic (Xinhua 2020). According to Save The Children charitable organization, an alarming 6.7 million more children could suffer from malnutrition this year due to the coronavirus. Therefore, we must take immediate action to end their hunger now.
