Millions of people living in parts of Africa lack the basic necessities of life.

An estimated 220 million people lack adequate nutrition. Approximately 1.6 million Africans died of malaria, tuberculosis and HIV-related illnesses in 2015.

Globally, 50% of children under five who die of pneumonia, diarrhea, measles, HIV, tuberculosis and malaria are in Africa, according to the World Health Organisation (WHO).
