Donation to Value village for CSQT donation

APPLY: 12/01/2020 - 01/01/2021

From 12/01/2020 to 01/01/2021, We will be partnering with Value Village. The total amount you buy or you donate to the value village, 5% of it donate to Africa.

You can bring items such as clothes, electrical appliances, etc. to the donation area of the value village near you.

CLOTHES FOR THE "NEIGHBORS"

APPLY: 12/06/2020 - 02/06/2021

Working with the Kamloops Hospice Association, CQST is receiving physical donations for providing new clothes and household appliances for people in Africa. All the items donated will be received outside the Thrift Store with physical distancing followed. We accept most gently used family tools, clothing, and furniture.

Donate items will be collected at Brock Shopping Center (#14-1800 Tranquille Rd, Kamloops, BC).

FEED AFRICA !

Next year, we will hold a food donation cooperating with Kamloops Food Banks. We will create a farming event where you can donate your money for Africa or take part in raising the seeds of fruit and vegetable trees that later will become one of our food resources for African families and people in need.

Thank you for your trust and support to our organization. Your help can change the lives of people in Africa.
