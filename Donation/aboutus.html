Who we are

Led by four young teenagers, CQST was born in the middle of the global hunger crisis. This organization's purpose is to offer tangibles and support, especially food, to people and families with a lack of nourishments in Africa. Working with other donate organizations and companies, CQST hopes to give out the best assistance and supply for communities in need of food.

Africa is a country that is very lacking in food and health. The people here have to eat nutritious food each person and that leads to the rate of malnutrition in Africa is leading the world. Because the health system is in short supply, the rate of people dying from disease, fever or malaria is at a high level.

The money you contribute to us, we will turn them into food, water and health for the people of Africa. We also opened a few more classes to eradicate illiteracy for children and people here.
